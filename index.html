Srimathi Sundaravalli Memorial School

The School Mission

The School imparts knowledge without any discrimination of caste, creed or community. The School strives to inculcate values and ethics into the minds of students, so as to enable them to build a noble society. The School moulds the students with utmost Patriotism, enabling them to value the Nation the most. The students are imbibed with the quality to respect the feelings of all others, especially the poor, down-trodden and the needy.

School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won First prize in Inter School Debate on Environment Pollution

The competition was held on March 17, 2021 at MCC School. Many students participated in the competition and presented their debates. Our school won the prize. Merit Certificates and a cup were awarded to us.

Inter School Sports Competition
Won Many prizes in Inter School Sports Competition

For this year's sports day, we had inter school competition. Students arrived from different schools within Chennai. We had sports week for a week. On the final day, certificates and medals were distributed to the winners. Our school won in many sports.

School Storytelling Competition
Students of Montassari won in the story telling competition

Children were given a few topics to choose from for their story telling competition. Each class is divided into two groups and prizes were distributed for the winner and runner-up from each group.

Latest News

News about Latest Additions

  • New School Dining Hall

    Our School has opened a new dining hall for students. Previously, each class had to wait for their turns for lunch. But now with this improved spacious dining hall almost half of the school can have lunch at the same time. We don't have to wait for a long time.

  • Smart Classes

    Smart classes are included recently for all classes.It helps to provide interactive education and it is easy to understand the concepts. It is a modern teaching method where students are provided with opportunities to keep up with the growing technical world.But it also made students to get used to mobile phones. Most of the classes wre uploaded in the website and students had to use internet to study them. It makes them exposed to all the available content of the internet both good and bad.

Ritesh Agarwal

Ritesh Agarwal

Ritesh Agarwal’s (Young Indian Entrepreneur) entrepreneurial journey is one of the most successful entrepreneur stories in India. After dropping out of college, he launched his first start-up Oravel Stays, which was a budget hotel chain that provided B&B. And that elevated to OYO rooms from when he realized no other service offered a room for a budget traveler. Begun with only 11 rooms in a Gurgaon hotel, OYO rooms is now a hub for elite hotel stays with 65000 rooms in about 5500 properties across India.

Trishneet Arora

Trishneet Arora

At the age of 25, Trishneet Arora (Young Indian Entrepreneur) is an author, a self-described friendly hacker and the founder and CEO of cyber security startup TAC Security Solutions. The India-based company performs Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing for corporates identifying weaknesses in their cyber security. He has received funding from angel investor Vijay Kedia and support from former VP of IBM, William May. In 2017, Arora was listed among the 50 Most Influential Young Indians by GQ Magazine, while August 25, 2017 was proclaimed “Trishneet Arora Day”, by the Mayor of Santa Fe, New Mexico.
